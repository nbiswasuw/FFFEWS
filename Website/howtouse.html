How to use the Flash Flood System Portal

Visualization of Forecast Flood Map

  • Select the forecast precipitation type used in the framework
  • Select the visualization parameter
  • Select the lead-time for which you want to visualize the datasets
  • Click "view" to visualize the flood map over the map.
  • Forecast date of flood map along map legend will be shown on top left corner of the map.

Visualization of Forecasted Precipitation

  • Select the forecast precipitation type used in the framework
  • Select the visualization parameter
  • Select the lead-time for which you want to visualize the datasets
  • Click view to view the precipitation raster dataset over the map.
  • Forecast precipitation date, type of precipitation and legend will be shown on top left corner of the map.

Visualization of Forecasted WL Timeseries of Stations

  • Yellow colored traingles are the locations of forecast WL stations
  • Click on any location to visualize station's timeseries (nowcast and forecast)
  • A timeseries chart will be shown for clicked station
  • Station's average and climatology data added, click on the title of series to plot them in the chart.

Visualization of Forecasted Inflow at the Boundaries

  • Blue colored circles are the locations of inflow boundaries.
  • Click on any of the location to visualize inflow timeseries (nowcast and forecast)
  • Nowcast and forecast discharge data will be shown for selected location
